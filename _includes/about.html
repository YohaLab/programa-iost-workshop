About

PlantSat is a contribution to the ongoing human assignment of Mars exploring, understanding and colonizing. Its goal is to study the growth of a suitable plant in orbit, attempting to replicate conditions that plants might find on Mars surface.

This means low gravity force and the high solar radiation due to its lack of a dense atmosphere. It is a joint project between the researcher Mr. Matthew Lehmitz, University of Chile, and the companies RocketLab and GomSpace.
